About UmmahFit

Welcome to UmmahFit — where your journey to becoming a stronger, more disciplined, and spiritually empowered individual begins. Founded with a passion for uplifting the Ummah, UmmahFit goes beyond a regular gym experience. It is a transformative environment where modern fitness meets Islamic values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop physically strong, mentally tough, and spiritually conscious individuals in the Muslim community. We believe that the body is an Amanah (trust) from Allah ﷻ, and it's our duty to take care of it. At UmmahFit, we’re here to motivate, educate, and uplift you—so you can strive for excellence in both this life and the Hereafter.

Meet Our Certified Trainers

Our trainers are not only fitness professionals but also mentors who understand your values. They are certified in physical training, sports nutrition, and also receive training in Islamic ethics to provide a wholesome coaching experience. From strength building to endurance, we cater to every goal with tailored fitness plans.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

We offer a clean, spacious, and well-equipped facility with the latest in gym equipment, functional training areas, a women-only workout zone, prayer areas, and healthy refreshment bars. All spaces are designed with Islamic etiquette and community comfort in mind.

Faith Meets Fitness

We understand the importance of balancing your Deen with your Dunya. That’s why our programs are aligned with the Sunnah lifestyle — including prophetic nutrition, Halal meal plans, and rest cycles based on Islamic principles. Our aim is to make fitness an act of worship.

Community & Brotherhood

UmmahFit is not just a gym; it's a community center for the youth and elders alike. We host regular motivational talks, Quran and fitness workshops, Islamic goal-setting sessions, and charity fitness challenges. We want every member to feel valued, empowered, and spiritually fulfilled.
